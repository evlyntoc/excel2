A PEOM ON CHARACTER

Here's a poem specially written for you girls based on today's class... Read, enjoy and apply to your life... Love you, girls

CHARACTER

Who I am and what I am
That's is who you are
Dear character
Like a garland of roses
Sweet and light
You adorned my crown
And light up my life
When I wear the good side of you
Am rewarded with smiles
Love and praises
But should I chose to care less
Dashing caution to the winds
Then a sack of rotten potatoes
Whose sight and smell disgust all
You become
Weighting heavily on my being

Oh Character
Though intangible yet glaringly visible
For consciously or unconsciously
You are who I am and what I am
Good or bad
Sweet or sour
The choice is mine to make
By my thoughts, words and deeds
For the world is silently watching
And will surely judge
My yesterday, today and tomorrow
By every of your displays
For they are who I am and what I am
My dear character
So I chose to intentionally stay good
That I might be the best me
The world can ever have
