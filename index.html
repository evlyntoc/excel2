I EXCEL MENTORING PROGRAM FOR PRETEENS AND TEENS

'I Excel' Mentoring Program is a training programme to groom preteen and teen girls. Girls that apply for our programme shall be trained on life skills useful to see them through adulthood. Curriculum includes but not limited to Leadership, Effective Communications, Identity, Character, Tourism, Etiquette, Arts, Science, IT and much more.

In June 2020 we started a Back to School competition for girls age 11-15 years and we are now at the 2nd stage of the competition. Sixty- seven preteen and teen girls applied. They would be engaged through WhatsApp training and quizzes before the best fifty successful candidates emerge and are giving their back to school packs. The overall winner becomes our brand ambassador. More information on the role of the brand ambassador would be updated as progress is made.
