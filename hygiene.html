✨🚺🚺🚺🚺🚺✨
▫️We will be looking at the concept of hygiene and it's practices.
*What exactly is good hygiene?*
🔹Sometimes what seems like a simple question can be a fairly complex issue if not well handled. However, we are going to answer this question in a way that it will be clear and easy to understand considering the age difference.
💠💠💠💠


✨🚺🚺🚺🚺✨
♦️ *DEFINITION* ♦️

🔹 _*Hygiene* is essentially how we keep our bodies clean and sanitary._ 👌
The cleanliness aspect serves two functions. *First,* being clean is necessary to function socially‼️ Other people, adults, in particular, expect that the people they are interacting with will be clean. _No person is an island🙄_ so being able to deal with other people is an essential skill for teens and adults alike. Good hygiene practices has *social and health benefits.*
*Secondly,* being clean allows us to be sanitary—maybe not completely free of germs, but mostly free of the germs🐙 that are harmful.


⭕🛀🛀🛀🛀🛀🛀⭕
✨ *BATHING* ✨
🔹Bathing twice is an important part of personal hygiene.
Bathing daily with soap 🧽and warm water helps prevent body odour .
If you have issue with sweating, an antiperspirant will be in order for you. If you shower twice daily and doesn't feel that deodorant is necessary, (observe a sniff test!), then it can be safely omitted.

*PAT YOURSELF USING A SOFT DRY TOWEL*
Pat your skin with a towel instead of rubbing vigorously.
Use a towel that is soft and completely dry. Wash your towel every two to three days. Don’t share your towel with anyone else.

*WEAR COTTON PANTIES* 👙
Cotton is a very breathable fabric, it allows any excess moisture from sweat to evaporate, instead of becoming a breeding ground for bacteria and unpleasant odours.
Washing your hair at least once a week using shampoo and conditioner.

✨🦷🦷🦷🦷🦷✨

♦️ *ORAL HYGIENE* ♦️

An attractive and healthy smile😁 is important when meeting people and making friends🤝. It can boost your confidence and help you feel good about yourself. 🤷‍♀️

📌 _Good dental hygiene will help prevent a variety of ills. Brushing your teeth removes some of the common bacteria that can cause bad breath._ 📌

However, poor dental hygiene can result in the following;
▪︎Bad breath 🗣
▪︎Stained teeth
▪︎Tooth decay
▪︎Gum disease
▪︎Tooth loss

⭕⭕⭕⭕⭕⭕⭕
*Remember*

you should brush your teeth twice a day – in the morning and before bed.

📌 *Floss regularly* 📌

Store your tooth brush in a clean, dry place and replace it regularly (once every three months and especially after illness)
💧💧💧💧💧


✨👗👗👗👗👗👗👗✨
🟣 *Hygienic Clothes* 🟣
Dirt and bacteria can build up on your clothes too. As part of your personal hygiene routine it is important to take care of your clothes by washing and
changing them regularly.

*Tips*
▪︎Change your clothes daily
▪︎Do not wear smelly clothes
▪︎ Do not wear damp clothes
▪︎Wash your clothes with soap or detergent rinse thoroughly.
▪︎Spread your clothes under direct sunlight



⭕⭕⭕⭕⭕⭕⭕

*Hygiene amist of covid*
_Pay attention to personal hygiene. Yes, I know you’ve heard all this a million times already. It bears repeating._
▪︎Wash your hands frequently 
with soap and water, scrubbing for at least 20 seconds, or use an alcohol-based hand sanitizer.
▪︎Don’t touch your face. 
▪︎Cover coughs and sneezes with the inside of your elbow or upper arm.
▪︎Keep your distance. 
▪︎Don’t hug or shake hands.
▪︎Make a conscious effort to avoid crowds.
  ▪︎Wear a mask. 


✨ *Conclusion* ✨

💃💃💃💃💃💃💃💃
Good hygiene practices doesn't have to be difficult. Once you have a good personal hygiene routine in place, it quickly becomes a habit. Remember keeping your body fresh and clean gives you _confidence and helps you to stay healthy_
