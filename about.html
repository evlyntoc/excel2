ABOUT 'I EXCEL'

UPDATES

The *'I Excel' Back to School Competition* July/ August 2020 came as a result of closure of schools and the thoughts that children may be home not really learning and since the book *Flower Girl* was published to promote the Girl Child education, it was important we took advantage of the Covid-19 lockdown to engage girls online.

The competition form was launched online in the month of June 2020 for one month and sixty- seven (67) girls applied.

A three(3weeks) online - WhatsApp classes was organized for them. They were taken through classes on Leadership, Communication, Public Speaking, Hygiene, Self Esteem, Career Choice, Character, Child Right and Self Confidence. The classes were very interactive.

During the end of the three (3) weeks classes, the participants were observed and would be scored/evaluated on the following:

i.Effective Participation ii. Conduct
iii. Early submission of assignments
iv. Good understanding of the topics taken
v. adherence to instructions
vi. Communications and
tenses.


Results shall be announced by Friday 4th September and gifts shall be distributed to winners on Saturday 26th September, 2020.

*The gifts will be categorized as follows*:

1. Overall winner

2. 1st runner up ( few participants)

3. 2nd runner up ( few participants)

4.General 1. all participants get branded *I Excel Back to School* exercise books and biro's.

5. General 2 - all participants get a copy of *Flower Girl* each
